Notable Achievements;

  • Dubbed the "Unkillable Demon King" by Cool, which has become a widely used title for him.
  • Is highly regarded for his LeBlanc play during an OGN Spring match against MVP Blue; he went 10-0-1 by 18 minutes and made MVP Blue surrender at 20 minutes.
  • Was both the Season MVP and the Mid Lane KDA leader in the HOT6iX Champions Summer 2013.
  • Awarded Best Mid Laner at 2013 Korean eSports Awards.
  • One of eight players to have defended their World Championship successfully, the only one to have done it twice.
  • He is the only player to have won World Championship five times (2013, 2015, 2016, 2023, 2024).
  • Has the highest number of appearances in finals in the history of the World Championship, with 7 in total (2013, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2022, 2023 and 2024).
  • He is the oldest player to ever win the World Championship, at 28 years, 5 months, and 27 days.
  • He is the first player to have been awarded the Worlds MVP twice.
